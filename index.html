About ocean

Ocean is a lovely new software that enables the audience to feel like they are at the ocean. Ocean produces calming sounds and displays an immersive video of the ocean.

This new software contains things like sunset/sunrise options, cloudy and rainy options, or just a simple ocean background.

Ocean includes free download and two free options for those who decide to launch this new software. There is an offered discount for those who purchase within the first week of release!
